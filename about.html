About Us

At CheesyPC, we’re passionate about bringing innovative, user-friendly Android applications to life. With over 200,000 users and counting, our mission is to create apps that not only simplify daily tasks but also enrich your digital experience. Whether it's staying updated with the latest bus schedules, enjoying timeless Tamil music, or exploring a vast collection of Tamil books and novels, our apps cater to a wide array of user needs.

Founded with the vision of making high-quality technology accessible to everyone, CheesyPC focuses on building apps that are intuitive, reliable, and designed to provide real value. Our growing portfolio on the Google Play Store demonstrates our commitment to delivering tools that help people in their everyday lives—be it for commuters, music lovers, or those who follow their daily horoscope.

Our users are at the heart of everything we do. That’s why we constantly strive to improve our apps with new features, performance updates, and improved user interfaces. We believe that technology should work for you, making life simpler, more enjoyable, and a little bit more "cheesy."

What Sets Us Apart:

  • Trusted by Over 200,000 Users: Our apps are relied on by a growing community of users for their practicality and ease of use.
  • User-Centered Design: We prioritize simplicity, functionality, and a seamless user experience.
  • Diverse App Portfolio: From Tamil literature and music to transit apps and horoscopes, we offer a wide range of solutions for different interests.
  • Commitment to Quality: Continuous updates based on user feedback ensure our apps stay current, reliable, and engaging.

At CheesyPC, we’re not just developers—we’re problem solvers and innovators. We take pride in listening to our users and evolving our products to meet their needs. Thank you for being part of our journey as we continue to develop apps that make life easier, more enjoyable, and a little bit more fun!
